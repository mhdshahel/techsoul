About Alnoor Hospital

Alnoor Hospital is a leading healthcare provider, dedicated to delivering compassionate and comprehensive medical services. Established with a mission to offer world-class care with a patient-centered approach, we have grown into a trusted name in the region, serving thousands of patients each year.

Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with modern infrastructure and advanced medical technologies. We offer a wide spectrum of services including emergency care, general medicine, surgery, pediatrics, cardiology, neurology, and more. Our experienced medical professionals and support staff are committed to excellence and are available 24/7 to meet the needs of our patients.
